Our Story

The Burger Box motto is, "Good Food and a Smile... That's Roadhouse Style", - great food, cooked on the premises in the original Roadhouse way, using the freshest ingredients of the highest quality. Quick, friendly service in a comfortable setting, our generous portions and competitive pricing make for great value for money.

The establishment, where the original Burger Box Roadhouse is today, began sixty years ago in Krugersdorp. It was a fast food store in the 50's and became a roadhouse in the 60's. During the 1980's it took the name Burger Box and evolved into the business it is today. With 3 branches now operating in Krugersdorp, Benoni, and Centurion. The Burger Box brand is spreading its wings and branching into Montana soon. Look out for new stores opening in the near future.
